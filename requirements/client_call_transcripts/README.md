# Client Call Transcripts

Meeting transcripts and call summaries from client interactions.

## Purpose

Maintain verbatim or near-verbatim records of all client meetings to ensure accurate requirement capture and provide audit trail.

## Content Types

- Project kickoff meetings
- Requirements gathering sessions
- Weekly status updates
- Sprint reviews and demos
- Ad-hoc clarification calls
- Technical discussion meetings

## Naming Convention

`YYYYMMDD_[MEETING_TYPE].md`

Examples:
- `20250922_project_kickoff_part1.md`
- `20250924_requirements_gathering_session_2.md`
- `20251007_weekly_update_week_2.md`
- `20251010_brd_clarifications_meeting.md`

## Best Practices

1. **Timeliness**: Upload transcripts within 24 hours of meeting
2. **Accuracy**: Include verbatim quotes for critical decisions
3. **Attendees**: List all participants with roles
4. **Action Items**: Clearly mark action items and owners
5. **Follow-up**: Link to follow-up emails or worksheets
6. **Decisions**: Highlight key decisions made during meeting
7. **Timestamps**: Include meeting date/time and duration

## Template Structure

```markdown
# [Meeting Type] - [Date]

**Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
**Time**: HH:MM - HH:MM [Timezone]
**Duration**: X hours
**Attendees**: 
- Name (Role, Company)
- Name (Role, Company)

## Agenda
1. Topic 1
2. Topic 2

## Discussion

### Topic 1
[Transcript/Notes]

### Topic 2
[Transcript/Notes]

## Decisions Made
- Decision 1
- Decision 2

## Action Items
- [ ] Action item - @Owner - Due: YYYY-MM-DD
- [ ] Action item - @Owner - Due: YYYY-MM-DD

## Next Steps
- Next meeting scheduled for: YYYY-MM-DD
- Topics for next meeting
```
